How to play LEXISPEED (Speed Version)

🟦 1. OBJECTIVE

LEXISPEED is all about speed, so forget about anything other than trying to form the 🎯 Target Word displayed at the top of the game as quickly as possible by moving the falling blocks sideways before they land at the bottom. The target word must be formed on the same level, with no gaps or incorrect letters in between. Move unwanted letters to the side, or make them disappear by following the block matching rules. REMEMBER, THERE'S A NEW WORD EVERY DAY!

🕹️ 2. CONTROLS

🖥️ Desktop: Use the ⬅️ Left Arrow, ➡️ Right Arrow, and ⬇️ Down Arrow keys to move the blocks.

🔄 Spacebar: Drop the block instantly.

📱 Mobile: Use the on-screen arrows to control the blocks.

🟢 3. BLOCK MATCHING RULES

🔤 Match Letters: Touching blocks with the same letter will disappear.

🎨 Match Colors: Touching blocks with the same color will disappear.

⚠️ BUT: Blocks that are part of the Target Word never disappear.

📊 4. LEADERBOARD

If your time is fast enough, you'll get to add your name to the 🏅 Leaderboard.

Click 📤 SHARE to challenge your friends and show off your time!

📊 5. PLAY NOW!

How to play LEXISTRAT (Strategy Version)

🟦 1. OBJECTIVE

LEXISTRAT is all about strategy, so take your time while trying to form the 🎯 Target Word displayed at the top of the game by moving falling blocks sideways before they land at the bottom. The target word must be formed on the same level, with no gaps or incorrect letters in between. REMEMBER, THERE'S A NEW WORD EVERY DAY!

🕹️ 2. CONTROLS

🖥️ Desktop: Use the ⬅️ Left Arrow, ➡️ Right Arrow, and ⬇️ Down Arrow keys to move the blocks.

🔄 Spacebar: Drop the block instantly.

📱 Mobile: Use the on-screen arrows to control the blocks.

🟢 3. BLOCK MATCHING RULES

🔤 Match Letters: Touching blocks with the same letter will disappear.

🎨 Match Colors: Touching blocks with the same color will disappear.

⚠️ BUT: Blocks that are part of the Target Word never disappear.

🏆 4. SCORING

✅ Points for Matches:

  • 🔤 Matching Letters: +150 points
  • 🎨 Matching Colors: +100 points
  • 💥 Matching Both (Letter + Color): +300 points
  • ⚠️ BUT: You don't get points for matching blocks that are part of the Target Word.

⬆️ Grid Placement:

  • Top Grid: x2.0 points
  • Middle Grid: x1.5 points
  • Bottom Grid: x1.0 points

📊 5. LEADERBOARD

If your score is high enough, you'll get to add your name to the 🏅 Leaderboard.

Click 📤 SHARE to challenge your friends and show off your score!

💡 6. TIPS FOR A HIGHER SCORE

  • Build higher on the grid for bigger multipliers.
  • Take your time: there is no bonus for building the Target Word faster.
