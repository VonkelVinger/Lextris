How to Play Leximax

Try to form the Target Word at the top of the game by moving the falling blocks sideways before they fall into place at the bottom.

Use the arrow keys and spacebar on your desktop device or the arrows on the screen on your mobile device to move the blocks or to drop them to the bottom immediately.

If touching blocks match in letter or in colour, both will disappear from the grid, but only if neither letter is part of the target word, or if the Target Word has been formed with the blocks on the same level and with no spaces or incorrect letters between them.

If your time is fast enough, you get to add your name to the leaderboard. Have fun!
