How to Play Leximax

📖 How to Play LEXIMAX! 🚀 🟦 1. Objective Try to form the 🎯 Target Word displayed at the top of the game by moving falling blocks sideways before they land at the bottom. 🕹️ 2. Controls 🖥️ Desktop: Use the ⬅️ Left Arrow, ➡️ Right Arrow, and ⬇️ Down Arrow keys to move the blocks. 🔄 Spacebar: Drop the block instantly. 📱 Mobile: Use the on-screen arrows to control the blocks. 🟢 3. Block Matching Rules 🔤 Match Letters: Touching blocks with the same letter will disappear. 🎨 Match Colors: Touching blocks with the same color will disappear. ⚠️ Special Rule: Blocks that are part of the Target Word won't disappear until the word is fully formed on the same level with no gaps or incorrect letters. 🏆 4. Scoring System ✅ Points for Matches: 🔤 Matching Letters: +150 points 🎨 Matching Colors: +100 points 💥 Matching Both (Letter + Color): +300 points 🔗 Chain Bonuses: Clear multiple matches in a row to increase your Chain Bonus Multiplier. Second Chain: x1.2 Third Chain: x1.4 And more! ⬆️ Grid Placement: Top Grid: x2.0 points Middle Grid: x1.5 points Bottom Grid: x1.0 points ⏱️ Time Bonus: Under 30 seconds: x1.5 multiplier Under 60 seconds: x1.2 multiplier 🎯 Target Word Bonus: Completing the Target Word rewards you with a massive point bonus! 📊 5. Leaderboard If your score is high enough, you'll get to 🏅 add your name to the Leaderboard. Click 📤 SHARE to challenge your friends and show off your score! 💡 6. Pro Tips for High Scores: Plan moves to create Chain Reactions. Match Letters and Colors simultaneously. Build higher on the grid for bigger multipliers. Complete the Target Word as quickly as possible. 🎮 Ready to Play? Hit START and see if you can climb to the top of the Leaderboard! 🥇✨
