Why today's word is hard to spell

"Occurred" is challenging because it contains doubled consonants ('c' and 'r') that don't follow typical doubling patterns. The 'cc' comes from Latin 'occurrere', while the 'rr' follows the rule of doubling consonants after stressed short vowels. Remembering "one C before, two Cs after O" and "two Rs after U" can help with the spelling.
