About Today's Word

Today's word is the noun OCTAVE. According to Dictionary.com, in music it can refer to: 1. a tone on the eighth degree from a given tone. 2. the interval encompassed by such tones. 3. the harmonic combination of such tones. 4. a series of tones, or of keys of an instrument, extending through this interval.
