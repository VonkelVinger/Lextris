Why the word from 26 January 2025 is hard to spell

The word "business" is tricky because it doesn't follow typical English spelling patterns. It derives from "busy" + "-ness," but keeps the 'i' instead of changing 'y' to 'i' before adding the suffix. The spelling rule that helps is remembering it contains "bus" (like a vehicle) plus "i" plus "ness" - breaking it into these memorable chunks makes the spelling more intuitive..
