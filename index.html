HERE'S HOW TO PLAY

  • 1. Move the falling blocks horizontally before they drop to the bottom to create the target word shown at the top.
  • 2. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard or the arrows below the grid on your mobile device to move the blocks.
  • 3. Press the spacebar (desktop) or the down arrow (mobile) to drop a block faster.
  • 4. If a block falls on top of or next to another block with a matching letter or color, both blocks will disappear from the grid, BUT letters that are part of the target word never disappear.
  • 5. The game is over when there is no more room for further blocks to fall.
  • 6. Win by creating the target word as quickly as you can.
