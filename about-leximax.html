About Leximax

Leximax is a free web-based word game inspired by the likes of Tetris and Wordle. Choose between LEXISPEED, where it's all about forming the Target Word as quickly as possible, or LEXISTRAT, where strategic play will earn you the maximum points before you form the Target Word.

Whichever version you prefer, playing LEXIMAX will improve your reading speed, hand-eye coordination and English spelling skills. If you enjoy our game, please tell all your friends, and help us keep it free by supporting our advertisers and other projects that we might feature on the site from time to time.
